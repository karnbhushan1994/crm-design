Step one

Schedule intro call

The first step is to fill out a short application and select a time on my calendar for your free intro call.

Don't worry––this is not a sales call. If we both feel like working together is a good fit, we can discuss that on the call. If not, I'll direct you to someone who can help.

Step two

Review proposal

After we've discussed your business and website needs, I'll put together a detailed project proposal.

This proposal will outline the scope of work, milestones, deliverables, terms of payment, project timeline, as well as a quote for the entire project.

Step three

Sign and deposit

TAfter we've agreed on the project scope, expectations, and timeline, the last step is to sign the proposal and submit payment.

I offer two payment plans to accommodate multiple budgets. Clients can choose to either pay in two installments or save 5 percent by paying upfront in full.
